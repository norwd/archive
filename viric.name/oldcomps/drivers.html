Device Drivers

This section pretends to hold drivers to old devices hard to find in the web.

Network Interface Cards (NICs)

  • UM90008F card. The UM90008F is the main chipset. 16-bit ISA 10Mbps card, with BNC and RJ45 connectors. PNP and Jumperless configurations.
  • UM90003AF card. The UM90003AF is the main chipset. 16-bit ISA 10Mbps card, with BNC and RJ45 connectors. PNP and Jumperless configurations.
  • RTL8029 card DISK#1 DISK#2 The RTL8029 is the main chipset. Usually PCI 10Mbps card, with BNC and RJ45 connectors. PNP and Jumperless configurations.
  • 3COM 509 Etherlink-III Floppy-1 16-bit ISA 10Mbps card, with BNC and RJ45 connectors. Only jumperless configuration.

Mice

  • Mouse driver , the famous imouse.com pack.

Block Devices

  • DOS Iomega ZIP-100 driver Driver for the first version of the Iomega Zip drive (100Mb disks).
  • GS CD-ROM driver This should work for all ATAPI-compatible IDE CD-ROM drives. It also supplies mscdex.exe.
