Grundig FR-200 AM/FM/SW Receiver

A lower-range AM/FM/SW unit with a rechargable NiCD battery pack and a handcranked dynamo for charging. Includes a flashlight. Tunes 88-108 MHz FM Broadcast, 530-1710 kHz AM broadcast, 3200-7600 kHz and 9200-22000 kHz shortwave coverage.

Can be powered from rechargable battery, 2 AA cells, or 4.5v external source. The manual claims 40-60 minutes of radio operation after a brisk crank of 120 RPM for 90 seconds.

Antenna is 65 cm telescoping whip; no jack for external antenna. I can receive several strong SW stations, but not getting WWV at 1700 localtime. AM and FM commercial broadcast reception is very good, and there's a swell carrying case.
