Pryme PR-460 ClearConnect Handie-Talkie

Manual

The PR-460 is a generic UHF transceiver capable of working anywhere in the 440-470 MHz range, simplex or duplex. It includes CTCSS endode and decode. Transmission power is 1/2/4 Watts with 12v battery pack or 13.8 V external power, 0.5/1/2 watts with a 7.2 V battery pack.

Be careful not to confuse this with the SportConnect which is little more than a hyper-powerful FRS unit supporting only the Call/Emergency channel and channels 1-7 above. 462.675 is the GMRS emergency and traveller's aid channel. 462.650 and 462.700 may not be used near the Canadian border.
