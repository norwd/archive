Alinco DJ-C5T Dual Band Transceiver

  • Transmits on 2 meters, 144-148 MHz, and 70 cm, 420-450 MHz, FM
  • Extended receive, 118-136 MHz AM and 136-174 MHz FM
  • output power: 300 mW

I carry this in my pocket when I'm at work. It weighs 3 ounces, so I just forget about it until I need it.

CTCSS encode on all the popular tones, but decode only up to 156.7 Hz. (Size does matter!). Internal Lithium-Ion battery.
