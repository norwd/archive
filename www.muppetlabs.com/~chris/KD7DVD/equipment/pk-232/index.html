AEA PK-232MBX Multimode Data Controller


This is the classic data controller, found covered in dust at fine hamfests and flea markets everywhere. So venerable that a living descendant exists, the Timewave PK-232/PSK

Does packet at 300 and 1200 baud. Does RTTY, AMTOR/SITOR, PACTOR and CW. Includes a maildrop that works in Packet, PACTOR and AMTOR modes, if you decide you want a personal mailbox accessible from anywhere in the world.

Used for HF data work, and monitoring marine information broadcasts.
