Realistic HTX-100 10-meter Transceiver

  • Transmits and receives USB and CW from 28.0 - 29.7 MHz
  • output power: 5 and 25 watts
  • Includes noise blanker, RIT and 10 channel memory
Intended for use by entry-level hams back when entry-level still meant 5 WPM Morse Code. Unlike Radio Shack's newer entry into the 10-meter market, this radio lets you do CW.

Right now this radio is idle, but I soon hope to have it in my car taking advantage of the good ground wave propagation properties of the 10-meter band.
