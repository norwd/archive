December 2000 Albany Open Shoot

There a few things as fun as shooting Class III weapons, and thankfully I live near a state where they are legal. Twice a year, a range in Albany, Oregon holds a full-auto Open Shoot. I went this past December with my buddies from the WSI Rifle League and we had a great time.
