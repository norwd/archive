Atom's Last Day

December 15th, 2000

On 12.15.2000, my employer AtomFilms announced it was merging with shockwave.com, eventually shutting down the Seattle office and moving operations down to San Francisco. The news was greeted with the most appropriate response: heavy drinking at the Owl-n-Thistle. Luke Jennings, as usual, had a camera.
