Albany Machine Gun Competitions

Oregon is a Class III state, God bless it, and there's a range down in Albany that holds monthly Full-Auto competitions. Trust me, it's probably harder than you think. Spraying bullets into a berm is one thing, but having to use as little ammo as possible and quickly hit targets [don't forget to control that muzzle rise] under time pressure, etc, etc, is another. Suffice to say, it's a lot of fun and I'm only decently good at it. My goal is to just be able to hold my own in a competition, and not be brushed off as being good ... "for a girl."
