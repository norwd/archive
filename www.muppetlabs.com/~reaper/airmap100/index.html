July 17, 2001

I just received my new Airmap 100, and will be posting a review here in the next few weeks.

Quick first impressions, though:

  • Nicely packaged
  • Comes with all the accessories you'd likely need
  • The data cable I got was broken, and a quick, painless phone call to the tech support line yielded a competent tech who said a new cable is in the mail asap
  • Interface is easy enough that you don't need the manual for much
