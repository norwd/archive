Images of the Icom IC-735

These pictures were taken with an Apple QuickTake 200 digital camera, and cropped and sized with xv. This radio is one that I bought from an estate sale, sticky with smoker's tar and with half the controls melted. I replaced several of the control buttons and knobs, cleaned off the tar, and had the radio professionally aligned at Malcom Technical Service. It's now working quite well for me, mostly on 10m at the moment. (You just have to ignore that the VFO knob is no longer round, due to melting a bit...)
