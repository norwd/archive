Singer 27k pictures

Singer tells me that this machine is a model 27k, made between January and June 1903, in Clydebank, Scotland. I took these pictures with a borrowed Apple QuickTake 200 digital camera, and adjusted the contrast and brightness on a few to bring out detail. Click on the thumbnails to bring up a larger image.

I also have some links to other sewing machine sites which I found useful when researching this machine.
