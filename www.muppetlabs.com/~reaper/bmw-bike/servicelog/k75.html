2001
Feb 20, 2001 11,200 New rear tire Ian Johnston/Aurora Suzuki I noticed an interesting "bump-bump" feeling while riding slowly -- pulled over to discover a self-tapping 3mm screw embedded deeply in my rear tire. Replaced it with another ME55 Metronic.
Mar 9, 2001 11,631 New clutch, spline lube, level II service Cascade BMW I brought the bike in for a spline lube, and decided at the service desk to have them do a level II service as well (replace all conceivable fluids and adjust everything else). I got a call two days later (they were short-staffed) that the clutch only had 1/4 of its meat left (hard driving by previous owner?) -- I figured I could afford it, so better to do it now, than later when I might not be able to afford it. Voila, new clutch. (And $1050 less in pocketbook.) Mileage was noted as 11361 on the receipt, but was over 11,500 when I brought it in -- was about 11,630ish when I picked it up.
Mar 9, 2001 11,640 New front brake pads Ian Johnston Replaced stock Brembo pads with EBC's. Hopefully this will improve initial bite. Fluid which came back into the reservoir was dark brown, makes me wonder how well Cascade really changed the fluid.
