This is a page of my thoughts, facts, pictures and whatnot of the BMW R65 LS, K75/3A and R100 "S" (it used to be an RT).

The pictures are (almost) all as taken by me, of bikes that I've either owned or had a chance to ride for a day (service loaners, usually).
