I occasionally discover that there is very little organized information about a collection of something that interest me. And so I wind up gather and organizing the information myself.

  • Martin Gardner's Collections of "Mathematical Games" Columns
    Martin Gardner wrote a monthly column on recreational mathematics for nearly thirty years. The collections of his columns span fifteen titles.
  • The Incredible Collections of Gustave Verbeek
    Gustave Verbeek was an illustrator and cartoonist at the start of the 20th century. His most remarkable cartoon strip was called "The Upside-Downs of Little Lady Lovekins and Old Man Muffaroo".
  • The Complete Anna Homler Discography
    Anna Homler is a vocalist who sings in a nonexistent language. Homler's own releases are scattered across a number of independent labels, and in addition to this she has collaborated with a wide variety of others artists. This page attempts to provide a list of all of her recorded appearances.
