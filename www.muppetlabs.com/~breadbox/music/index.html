  • The Complete Anna Homler Discography
    Anna Homler is a vocalist who sings in a nonexistent language. Because you cannot understand what her lyrics mean, her music invites you to listen to the singing and the words as purely musical expressions. Homler's own releases are scattered across a number of independent labels, and in addition to this she has collaborated with a wide variety of others artists. This page attempts to provide a list of all of her recorded appearances.
