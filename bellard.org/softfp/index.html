SoftFP Library

Features

  • Support of the IEEE 754-2008 32/64/128 bit floating point types.
  • Support of the 4 elementary operations plus square root, fused multiply-add, minimum, maximum, conversion to and from 32/64/128 bit signed or unsigned integers.
  • RISC-V FPU specifics are supported by default.
  • 128 bit floating point type and 128 bit integers relying on the C compiler __int128 type.
