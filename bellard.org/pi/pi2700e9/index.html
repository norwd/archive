Pi Computation Record

By Fabrice Bellard

On December 31st, 2009, about 2700 billion decimal digits of Pi were computed using a single desktop computer. It was the World Record for the computation of Pi until the record of 5 trillion digits of Alexander J. Yee & Shigeru Kondo on August 2, 2010.
