S-M COMICS - CHARACTERS

Here are the ten characters from S-M Comics (1972-73). The characters were drawn from the digits 0-9. Numbers 1 (Hag) and 3 (Hag's Butler) were most common; the other characters appeared only infrequently.


1. Hag
2. George
3. Hag's Butler

4. Happy Joe
5. Plumber & Painter
6. Chinaman

7. Head Plumber & Painter
8. Frank N. Stein
9. Tallface

0. Druggist
