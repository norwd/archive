CURRENT EMPLOYMENT

NASA Goddard Space Flight Center

I've worked at the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center in Greenbelt, Maryland, since 1991. For about 10 years I worked on the flight software of the Hubble Space Telescope's DF-224 on-board computer. Nowadays I am doing research in space physics in the Interplanetary Physics Branch with the Cassini and MMS spacecraft.
