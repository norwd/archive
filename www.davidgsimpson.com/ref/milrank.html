UNITED STATES MILITARY OFFICER RANK INSIGNIA

 Insignia  Army, Air Force, Marines  Navy, Coast Guard 
 Six silver stars  General of the Armies  Admiral of the Navy 
 Five silver stars  General of the Army  Fleet Admiral 
 Four silver stars  General  Admiral 
 Three silver stars  Lieutenant General  Vice Admiral 
 Two silver stars  Major General  Rear Admiral (Upper Half) 
 One silver star  Brigadier General  Rear Admiral (Lower Half) 
 Silver eagle  Colonel  Captain 
 Silver oak leaf  Lieutenant Colonel  Commander 
 Gold oak leaf  Major  Lieutenant Commander 
 Two silver bars  Captain  Lieutenant 
 One silver bar  First Lieutenant  Lieutenant, Junior Grade 
 One gold bar  Second Lieutenant  Ensign 

Notes: The six-star rank exists only in the Army and Navy. The five-star rank exists only in the Army (General of the Army), Navy (Fleet Admiral), and Air Force (General of the Air Force). The four-star rank is the highest rank in the Marine Corps and Coast Guard.
