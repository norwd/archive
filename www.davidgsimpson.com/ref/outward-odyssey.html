OUTWARD ODYSSEY: A PEOPLE'S HISTORY OF SPACEFLIGHT

The following is a list of titles in the series Outward Odyssey: A People's History of Spaceflight, published by the University of Nebraska Press. The titles are listed here in publication order.
  1. Into That Silent Sea: Trailblazers of the Space Era, 1961-1965
  2. In the Shadow of the Moon: A Challenging Journey to Tranquility, 1965-1969
  3. To a Distant Day: The Rocket Pioneers
  4. Homesteading Space: The Skylab Story
  5. Ambassadors from Earth: Pioneering Explorations with Unmanned Spacecraft
  6. Footprints in the Dust: The Epic Voyages of Apollo, 1969-1975
  7. Realizing Tomorrow: The Path to Private Spaceflight
  8. The X-15 Rocket Plane: Flying the First Wings into Space
  9. Bold They Rise: The Space Shuttle Early Years, 1972-1986
  10. Wheels Stop: The Tragedies and Triumphs of the Space Shuttle Program, 1986-2011
  11. Go, Flight!: The Unsung Heroes of Mission Control, 1965-1992
  12. Infinity Beckoned: Adventuring Through the Inner Solar System, 1969-1989
  13. Fallen Astronauts: Heroes Who Died Reaching for the Moon
  14. Apollo Pilot: The Memoir of Astronaut Donn Eisele
  15. Outposts on the Frontier: A Fifty-Year History of Space Stations
  16. Come Fly with Us: NASA's Payload Specialist Program
  17. Shattered Dreams: The Lost and Canceled Space Missions
  18. The Ultimate Engineer: The Remarkable Life of NASA's Visionary Leader George M. Low
  19. Beyond Blue Skies: The Rocket Plane Programs That Led to the Space Age
  20. A Long Voyage to the Moon: The Life of Naval Aviator and Apollo 17 Astronaut Ron Evans
  21. The Light of Earth: Reflections on a Life in Space
  22. Son of Apollo
