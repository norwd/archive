Inverted low contrast style

Filter based solutions for those who like it dark (mostly, on dark sites effect is opposite), but don't like acid-trippy images and colors. Works reasonably smooth, unless there are lots of images.

css

html, img { -webkit-filter: invert(95%) hue-rotate(180deg); }
