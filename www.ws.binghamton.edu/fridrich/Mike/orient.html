Legend:

U = Top(Up) D = Down (Bottom)
R = Right   L = Left
B = Back    F = Front

Ls = LR'   Rs = RL'   Ra = RL = La
Ds = DU'   Us = UD'   Da = DU = Ua
Fs = FB'   Bs = BF'   Fa = FB = Ba

Ls' = Rs   Rs' = Ls   Ra' = R'L' = La'
Ds' = Us   Us' = Ds   Da' = D'U' = Ua'
Fs' = Bs   Bs' = Fs   Fa' = F'B' = Ba'

Inverting:  Read backwards and negate everything.

Reflecting: 
  over x axis replace
      B  - F'  F  - B'  R  - R'  L  - L'  D  - D'  U  - U'
      B' - F   F' - B   R' - R   L' - L   D' - D   U' - U
      B2 - F2  F2 - B2  R2 - R2  L2 - L2  D2 - D2  U2 - U2
      Bs - Bs  Fs - Fs  Rs - Ls  Ls - Rs  Ds - Us  Us - Ds
      Ba - Ba' Fa - Fa' Ra - Ra' La - La' Da - Da' Ua - Ua'

  over y axis replace
      R  - L'  L  - R'  F  - F'  B  - B'  D  - D'  U  - U'
      R' - L   L' - R   F' - F   B' - B   D' - D   U' - U
