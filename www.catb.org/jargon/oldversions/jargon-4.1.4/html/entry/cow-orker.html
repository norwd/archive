cow orker n.

[Usenet] n. fortuitous typo for co-worker, widely used in Usenet, with perhaps a hint that orking cows is illegal. This term was popularized by Scott Adams (the creator of Dilbert) but seems to have originated in a 1997 ir earlier scary devil monastery FAQ. Compare hing, grilf, filk, newsfroup.
