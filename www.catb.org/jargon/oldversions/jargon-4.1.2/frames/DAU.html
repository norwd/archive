DAU /dow/ n.

[German FidoNet] German acronym for Dümmster An­zu­neh­men­der User (stupidest imaginable user). From the en­gin­eer­ing-slang GAU for Grösster Anzunehmender Unfall, worst assumable accident, esp. of a LNG tank farm plant or something with similarly disastrous consequences. In popular German, GAU is used only to refer to worst-case nuclear acidents such as a core meltdown. See cretin, fool, loser and weasel.
