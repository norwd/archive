dentro /den'troh/

[ demoscene] Combination of demo (sense 4) and intro. Other name mixings include intmo, dentmo etc. and are used usually when the authors are not quite sure whether the program is a demo or an intro. Special purposes coinages like wedtro (some member of a group got married), invtro (invitation intro) etc. have also been sighted.
