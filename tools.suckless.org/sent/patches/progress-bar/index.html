Progress Bar

Description

This patch introduces a progress bar at the very bottom of non-image slides. Similar to the slide numbers patch, it may be useful for an audience to know how much more of a presentation they have to endure. One way of measuring this is to add a bar to the slides which indicate the presenter's progress based on slide count.
